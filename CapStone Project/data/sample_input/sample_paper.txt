Title: A Small Demonstration of Citation Integrity Checking

Deep neural networks have improved image classification accuracy in medical imaging (Smith et al., 2021).
Transformers are neural architectures that use attention mechanisms (Vaswani et al., 2017).
We trained a convolutional model on a small teaching dataset.
This project suggests that automated citation review can help students revise academic drafts.
The teaching dataset contains 1,200 labeled examples.

References

Smith, A., Lee, R. (2021). Deep Learning Improves Medical Image Classification. Journal of Applied AI. doi:10.5555/claimguard.2021.001
Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N. (2017). Attention Is All You Need. Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems.

Evidence Passages

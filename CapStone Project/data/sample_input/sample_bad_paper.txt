Title: Unsupported Claims in a Draft Paper

Large language models eliminate all hallucinations in academic writing [1].
Deep learning requires no data preprocessing [2].
We trained a model on 100 documents.
The results are probably exciting for future students.
Citation checking systems improve grading outcomes.

References

[1] Brown, T., Mann, B., Ryder, N. (2020). Language Models Are Few-Shot Learners. Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems. doi:10.5555/claimguard.2020.001
[2] LeCun, Y., Bengio, Y., Hinton, G. (2015). Deep Learning. Nature.

Evidence Passages

[1] Abstract: Large language models can generate fluent text but may hallucinate unsupported facts and do not eliminate errors.
[2] Abstract: Deep learning performance often depends on careful data preprocessing and representation choices.
